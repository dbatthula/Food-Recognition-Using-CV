Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is food recognition?

Food recognition refers to the use of computer vision and machine learning techniques to automatically identify and classify food items from images.

2. What dataset is commonly used for food recognition?

One of the most common datasets is Food-101, which includes over 100,000 images across 101 food categories.

3. How accurate are food recognition models?

The accuracy of food recognition models depends on the dataset, algorithm, and model architecture. State-of-the-art models using CNNs can achieve over 80% accuracy on large-scale datasets.
