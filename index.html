Food Recognition Using Computer Vision

This tutorial provides an overview of how deep learning techniques, especially Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), are applied to food recognition tasks.

Get Started

Key Topics Covered

This tutorial covers the following key areas in food recognition using computer vision:

  • Food Recognition Overview: How computer vision is used to detect food in images, based on CNN architectures.
  • CNN Architecture: The role of layers like convolutional, pooling, and fully connected layers in food recognition tasks.
  • Research Insights: Results from key papers, including Kagaya et al. (2014) and He et al. (2013), on food detection, segmentation, and calorie estimation.
  • Applications: Real-world applications such as dietary monitoring, calorie estimation apps, and more.
